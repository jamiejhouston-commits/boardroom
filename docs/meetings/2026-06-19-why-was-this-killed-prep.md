# Meeting Prep — Why Was This Killed?

Role: AgentsOrchestrator / General Manager
Status: Attendance confirmed
Subject: Next steps — why was this killed?

## What I will prepare

- A decision audit: when/where the idea was stopped, paused, descoped, or deprioritized.
- A root-cause review separating facts from assumptions.
- A recovery recommendation: revive, reshape, park, or formally kill.
- A pipeline-control correction so strong ideas are not lost without a written decision log.

## Deliverables I will bring

1. Decision timeline template.
2. Kill/hold/revive criteria.
3. Questions for the owner/team to identify the actual blocker.
4. Recommendation format for restarting the work if justified.
5. QA gate for any revived scope.

## Initial GM position

I will not assume the idea deserved to be killed. I will require evidence: spec status, task status, blockers, market reasoning, technical constraints, and who made the decision. If there is no documented decision, I will treat it as a pipeline failure and bring a corrective process.
