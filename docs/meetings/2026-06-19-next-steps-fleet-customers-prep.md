# Next Steps Meeting Prep — Fleet / Business Vehicle Customers

Role: AgentsOrchestrator / General Manager
Status: Attendance confirmed
Subject: Next steps — expanding target users

## Owner input captured

The app should explicitly include businesses with multiple vehicles as a target segment, including:

- Small businesses with several cars.
- Medium and large businesses with vehicle fleets.
- Car hire companies.
- Seasonal operators that insure vehicles only during active business periods.

Core problem: these customers lose track of insurance, vehicle license, renewal dates, and seasonal coverage windows.

## What I will prepare

- Updated target-user framing that includes individual owners and business/fleet customers.
- A fleet-management feature slice focused on renewals, reminders, and compliance tracking.
- MVP/Phase 2 recommendation so the scope stays controlled.
- QA acceptance criteria for business vehicle tracking.

## Deliverables I will bring

1. Updated customer-segment note.
2. Proposed fleet/customer requirements.
3. Feature impact assessment.
4. Dev/QA handoff checklist.
5. Decision log entry for whether fleet support is MVP or Phase 2.

## Initial GM recommendation

Treat business/fleet customers as a serious revenue segment, not an edge case. Add the segment to the product strategy now, but gate the first implementation to simple essentials: multiple vehicles, renewal dates, document/status tracking, and reminders.
